Mobile Friendly as a Requirement

Accesing websites through smartphone and phablets is already a trend. Websites should support mobile devices or provide good mobile experience in order to keep up with the present innovative world. Creativity incorporated into modern browsers has optimized mobile experience by providing user friendly features and diversified accesible platforms.

Dependency on Mobile Continues to Grow

The number of smartphones users has already outpaced that of desktop, tablets, and laptops users in the world. Just over 36% of the world's population was projected to use a smartphone by 2018, up from about 10% in 2011, according to recent statistics.

Android-Based Web Browsing is High

As smartphone usage continues to grow, Android-based mobile devices are now an important gateway to connect to the online world for the average person. Your websites needs to pass the mobile-friendly test to enhance the experience of users interacting with your site on an Android device.

What Exactly Is a Mobile-Friendly Site, Anyway?

mobile-friendly site is one that loads and opens fast on a mobile device and is easy to read, navigate and use on the small mobile screen
