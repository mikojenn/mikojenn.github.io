    As a chemical engineer specializing in the research and development of cleaning products for a multi-national company, Miracle co-founder Alex found that the cleaning products in the markets were either too expensive or inconsistent in quality. So together with his wife Suzette, they started a company dedicated to bringing high quality yet affordable cleaning products to everyone.


    Miracle’s start in 2005 was difficult to say the least. With the advent of new brands and massive advertising campaigns from large corporations, it was even more difficult to reach out to people.


    But with hardwork and dedication, Miracle grew from husband & wife mixing chemicals in their backyard, ten gallons at a time to make dishwashing liquids, to a team of chemists, engineers and others working in a proper factory with the right equipment making different varieties of dishwashing liquids, detergents and other cleaning products.


    Miracle’s team continues to reach even more people by doubling their efforts in bringing everyone higher quality cleaning products at the best possible prices.
